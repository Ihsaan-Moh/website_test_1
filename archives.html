AANLEGTOETS

IEEE SB GECI conducted “AANLEGTOETS” a multi level event which was a real entertainer. The event was divided into three levels. The first level was a jigsaw puzzle level- “PUZZLE MANIAC”. Here the participants were given 6 jigsaw puzzles which they had to solve and take a screenshot of the same and upload it in the form provided. The top 30 attendees who cleared all the puzzles in the minimum amount of time were selected to the next level. The second level was logical reasoning and aptitude questionnaire- “RACKING BRAIN”. The contestants who finish all 9 questions in the least amount time moved on to the third and final round. The last round was called “JUST AMINUTE” were the remaining 10 contestants were given a topic on which they had to speak on for one minute on the spot. The judgment criteria were the presentation and the points confined in that minute. This was really a different and exciting event. …
